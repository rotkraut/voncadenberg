Milky Way

Why Milky Way?

We decided to draw inspiration from the universe and from heavenly bodies when naming our apartments. This is how we want to remember the important astronomer from Trentino Giuseppe Antonio Slop von Cadenberg.

As you well know our Galaxy, the Milky Way is a so-called spiral galaxy and the spiral staircase that connects the two floors of the apartment reminds us of it.

In the mansard rooms upstairs, from the roof windows, you can fall asleep watching the stars, just like Slop.
