La Specola

La specola is a bright apartment on the top floor of the house. The large window allows the view of the surrounding mountains.

Why this name?

We like to think that Giuseppe Slop, previous owner of the house and well-known astronomer, used this very spot as observatory (in Italian, "osservatorio" or "specola") to gaze at the stars.

The apartment has a kitchen and a bathroom with shower. Just like Slop, you can fall asleep under the glow of the stars that filters through the windows of the attic ceiling in the bedroom, dreaming of new discoveries.
