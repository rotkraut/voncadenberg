Welcome to your new holiday home!

The Von Cadenberg is a new family run Bed and Breakfast that will be open all year round.

Here you can enjoy the fresh mountain air, go on wonderful hiking and biking tours or relax at the lake surrounded by nature. If you love snow and skiing, the Von Cadenberg is the place for you: Monte Bondone offers everything winter sports enthusiasts desire! All this just 10 minutes away from the city of Trento.

At the moment we are renovating the apartments to offer you a cosy house where you can spend memorable holidays.

For more information about the apartments, the area and to get a taste of what you will find at the Von Cadenberg, please visit the other sections of our website. See you soon!

We are pet-friendly

Your four-legged friends are welcome here! We ask them (and their owners) to be respectful of the spaces of the Von Cadenberg.
