Deu Ita

We are Corrado and Eugenia and soon we will be ready to welcome you here at the Von Cadenberg.

We lived here ourselves when the house still needed renovation and most of Cadine was made up of old farmhouses.

As real "Trentini" (inhabitants of the region Trentino), we love the mountains and tranquility. To us, Cadine has always been a place where we can relax and get away from the daily stress of the city.

Our blue house has always been the starting point for daily excursions to the green mountains that surround the village.

Here you will have endless possibilities: you can go on fun family tours (we also have enjoyed unforgettable days with our children here), spend days at lakes in the surroundings, go on cultural visits (museums, historical sites, the astronomical observatory "Terrazza delle stelle" or just enjoy and relax in the garden of the Von Cadenberg.

We are certainly not the only ones who appreciate the mountains and their beauties: that's why we decided to share our unique house with you, so that you will have a comfortable place from where you can explore and discover these marvellous places!
