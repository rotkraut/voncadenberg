Where

The Von Cadenberg is located in the historic part of Cadine in Via del Capitello 35.

10 minutes away from the city of Trento...

In the heart of the region Trentino, Cadine is only 10 minutes away from Trento.

The tranquility and refreshing coolness of the place will make you feel far away from the noise of the city, but at the same time you will be able to reach shops, museums and attractions in no time at all!

Surrounded by nature...

Cadine is the perfect starting point for many mountain walks of any difficulty level.

In summer you will also have the chance to venture out by bike or explore the lakes of Trentino. Whereas in winter you could spend a wonderful skiing holiday with family or friends.

Only 2 minutes walk from the Von Cadenberg, you will find a supermarket (Famiglia Cooperativa) and a restaurant, La Stube del Galletto which offers typical dishes and excellent beer!
